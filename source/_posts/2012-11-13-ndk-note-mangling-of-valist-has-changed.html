最近搞NDK，一切还算顺利，只是每次ndk-build都会提示：

In file included from jni/fskmodule.cpp:7:0:
/xxxx/usr/include/jni.h:592:13: note: the mangling of 'va_list' has changed in GCC 4.4

解决办法，修改 Android.mk，加一行：

LOCAL_CFLAGS = -Wno-psabi
